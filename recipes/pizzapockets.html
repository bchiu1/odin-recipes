Pizza Pockets

Pizza Pockets are a good choice when you're in a hurry!

Pizza Pockets are delicious and I don't care what anyone says. It's ready in 2 minutes and you can take it with you when you leave the house.

Are you ready to cook a delicious meal? It's easy! Here's a list of the ingredients you will need to prepare:

  • frozen pizza pockets
  • a microwave

Steps

Now that you have all the ingredients, follow these simple directions in order to cook the quickest and most delicious meal of your life!
  1. put frozen pizza pockets into the microwave
  2. microwave for 2 minutes, then serve!
There you have it! Lunch or dinner is served!
