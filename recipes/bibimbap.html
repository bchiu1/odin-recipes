Bibimbap

Bibimbap is a popular dish in Korea made of beef, vegetables, and rice.

Bibimbap is a popular dish in Korea which can be eaten all year around. The ingredients are simple and the dish can be ready in as little as 5 minutes, if you know know to do it right.

Are you ready to cook a delicious meal? It's easy! Here's a list of the ingredients you will need to prepare:

  • cooked shredded beef
  • shredded carrots
  • bean sprouts
  • raw or cooked egg, uncooked yolk
  • shredded cucumber
  • shredded dried seaweed
  • sesame oil
  • gochujang (red pepper paste) for taste

Steps

Now that you have all the ingredients, follow these simple directions in order to cook the quickest and most delicious meal of your life!
  1. put cooked rice into a bowl
  2. add the shredded carrots, cucumber, bean sprouts, beef, dried seaweed, and egg
  3. mix together with spoon, making sure the ingredients are eventually distributed. If you are using a cooked egg, make sure the egg is broken into easily edible pieces, similar to the beef
  4. add some sesame oil while mixing for extra flavour, but don't put too much!
  5. lastly, put a little bit of red pepper paste for some added flavour. If you can not handle spicy food, don't put too much, or any at all! It's okay, the dish will still be delicious!
There you have it! Lunch or dinner is served!
